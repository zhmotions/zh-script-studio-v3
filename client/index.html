ZH Script Studio v3.0

Quick start

  1. 📂 Open — open a .docx, .txt or .srt script. It auto-reloads next time.
  2. Select text in the script.
  3. ✨ Titles dock — send it to your timeline.
  4. 💬 Captions dock — auto-subtitle from audio, or from your script.
  5. 🎬 Templates dock — browse animations.

Timeline buttons

  • ＋ Title — selected text → one editable, animated title at the playhead.
  • ≣ Batch — every selected line → its own title (each lasts Dur seconds).
  • ⚑ Marker — drops a timeline marker with the selected text as comment.

🎨 Mark text

Select text, then click a colour to colour it, the yellow swatch to highlight, or the last swatch to clear.

⚙ Display

Font (incl. Bengali), size, line spacing, text/background colour and zoom — in reading mode.

Works in

Adobe Premiere Pro & After Effects (25.0 and newer).
